Our story

Julien Limbach, former Belgian ice cream champion, and his partner Katrien Van Goethem just sprinkled a dash of magic onto the ice cream scene of Antwerp and Mortsel with Mitico Gelato. Julien, originally a chef, fell in love with the art of gelato a few years back. His passion propelled him to the top of his craft, clinching the Belgian Gelato Championship in 2021 with a daring porcini, black olive, and pistachio creation.

After the success of their previous parlor, Julien and Katrien started a new chapter with Mitico. Nestled on Statielei in Mortsel, Mitico offers classic flavors and creations with an Ecuadorian twist, honoring Katrien's roots. With a spacious, well-equipped lab, they have room to perfect their craft every day.
